Needing and Wanting

A fountain where the stream from a large, expensive faucet misses a small, cheap sink, and is caught by the garbage can, circulating endlessly.

This work was partially inspired by a recommendation from a feng shui practitioner I consulted: he suggested that a fountain be placed at the entrance to the exhibition.
