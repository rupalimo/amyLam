Make-Believe Bathroom

  • 2020
  • artwork
  • collaboration
  • publicbathroom.online
  • Exhibition Documentation (SFU Gallery)
  • Video of talk about toilets (with HaeAhn Kwon)
  • Washrooms for all sticker project in collaboration with TO Toilet Codes

  • Curated by Jenn Jackson.

    Web development by Naomi Cui. Graphics by Emerson Maxwell. Audio tours by Aliya Pabani.

When stuck at work, the bathroom can be an ideal place to hide or recover. I was asked to make an exhibition during the pandemic fall of 2020 for a gallery at Simon Fraser University; as a result, the Make-Believe Bathroom is an online bathroom modelled after the actual, physical bathroom closest to the gallery on campus. Users of Make-Believe Bathroom can talk to people in stalls next to them, check a phone, view and leave graffiti, leave a message for others, and flush the toilet.
