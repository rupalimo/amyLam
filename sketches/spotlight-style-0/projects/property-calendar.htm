Property Calendar

I kept a journal for thirteen months (2021-2022), writing down every time the topics of real estate, housing, and property came up in conversation and life.

Presented as a one-page-a-day calendar: every day of the exhibition, a gallery worker rips off the previous day, revealing a new entry.

Revised and published as a book, Property Journal, in 2024.
