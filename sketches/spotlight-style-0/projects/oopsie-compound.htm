Oopsie Compound

  • 2023
  • Artwork
  • Writing

  • Collaboration with HaeAhn Woo Kwon.

  • In the exhibition a small but comfy house and maybe a dog, Richmond Art Gallery (2023).

A model of a fantasy communal home made out of found objects and my childhood toys, imagined in collaboration with friend and artist HaeAhn Woo Kwon. Inspired by a phrase by HaeAhn: “A one-bedroom apartment inside a donut.”

The models comprise: You don’t have to die Library, ‘Til it runs clear Bathhouse, At the edge of Sleephouse, and Shared empty purse Theatre.
