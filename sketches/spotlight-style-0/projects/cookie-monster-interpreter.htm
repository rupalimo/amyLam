C**kie Monster *nterpreter

  • 2021
  • collaboration
  • performance
  • Watch performance (Drip-drop.tv)

  • Presented for the 11th Seoul MediaCity Biennale.

    Collaboration with Oliver Husain. With Aliya Pabani. Sound by Matt Smith/Prince Nifty. Assistance from Monica Moraru.

    Supported by Canada Council for the Arts.

The thing about puppets is that they always have the same face, it doesn’t change no matter what is happening around them. And that’s what’s fun about them, it’s kind of like this fantasy of not being constantly roiled by emotions.

A livestream lecture-performance about translation, cuteness, and the un-human (monsterdom). Based on a true story.
