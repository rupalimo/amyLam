Cactus Lives in Yogurt Container for Three Years

Hanging on both dead and living bamboo, The Pond is a newspaper Robin Simpson and I made together that records all kinds of very important daily facts and announcements from our life. This includes opinion letters we wrote that got rejected from other newspapers; local animal gossip; when we saw the solar eclipse earlier this year; a retelling of a Daoist story about The Great Clump (a.k.a. all creation); a memorial for a friend… I think The Pond is about time experienced together—how full it is—even when it feels like nothing around us is changing in any good way, when everything is stagnant.
